Annadanam – Feeding the Needy

Offering food is offering life. Join our sacred mission to nourish both body and soul.

What is Annadanam?

Annadanam – the act of offering food – is one of the most sacred and selfless forms of charity in Indian culture. At Hare Krishna Movement Chennai, we believe in the profound Vedic teaching:

“Annad bhavanti bhutani” – All living beings subsist on food.
— Bhagavad Gita 3.14

Through our Annadanam Seva, we serve sanctified, nutritious vegetarian meals to the underprivileged, children, and pilgrims. These meals are prepared in a clean, spiritually uplifting environment and distributed with love and humility.

Why Annadanam?

  • Feeds the body and soul – offered food becomes prasadam.
  • Inclusive service – no discrimination of any kind.
  • Sustainable impact – regular support for health and well-being.

How You Can Participate

You can sponsor meals, volunteer your time, or help us expand our infrastructure. Every contribution helps us serve more lives with compassion and dignity.
